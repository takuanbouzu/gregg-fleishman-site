01 — Furniture

Furniture

8 designs

Each chair began as a geometric problem. The proportions of the joints, the angle of the back, the span of the seat — all derived from the cubic diagonal ratios. In the early 1990s, MoMA New York, Yale, and the Art Institute of Chicago acquired pieces from this body of work.

02 — Pods & Panel Structures

Pods & Panels

3 designs

Planar geometric structures using integrated frame-and-panel systems in European birch plywood. Geodesic in spirit, cubic in mathematics. Each panel locks into its neighbors through the same slot-and-pin joinery that runs through all of Fleishman's work.

03 — Node Structures

Node Structures

4 designs

Nodal configurations built from small, CNC-cut components connecting at the vertices of the space-filling geometry. The Temple of Whollyness, built at Burning Man 2013, assembled a six-story central space from these parts in 15 days — without a single metal fastener.

04 — Sky Portal Series

Sky Portal

4 designs · ongoing

Emerging from late 2022, the Sky Portal Series returns to simplified slot-and-pin construction. Panels intersect at 90° angles, drawing from cubic polyhedra. The series creates a bridge between the terrestrial and celestial — from single portal structures to assemblies that can grow infinitely. The family continues to expand.

Gallery open at Sky Portal X · 201 S. Broadway, Los Angeles.
