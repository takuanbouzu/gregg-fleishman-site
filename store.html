License terms

Each file purchase grants a single-use license: one physical item may be fabricated from the cutting files. The files are for personal, non-commercial use. Commercial fabrication or reproduction for resale requires a separate commercial license — contact the studio.

Files are provided as .dxf and .dwg, sized for standard CNC cutting. Material specifications and assembly notes are included with each download.
